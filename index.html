PLEASED TO MEET YOU

Born in Argentina, Capsula are Rock 'n' Roll. The forever touring couple formed by Martin Guevara - wild front man and guitarist, and his Rock 'n' Roll soulmate Coni Lisica on bass - create huge waves of 70s rotten glam riffs (think Bowie and T-Rex) with blowing drops of South American psychedelia to transport you to their constellation of free and savage sounds.
