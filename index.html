Pleased to Meet You

Born in Argentina and honed in Bilbao, Spain, Capsula were destined to forge an artistic partnership, and have been inventing mysterious new music with an ancient soul together for more than two decades. Wild front man and guitarist Martin Guevara, and Coni Lisica, his Rock 'n' Roll soulmate on bass, are forever touring the world, spreading their garage guitar gospel. Capsula channels the likes of Bowie and T-Rex, creating sparkling waves that crest in 70s glam riffs, dripping with South American psychedelia. The Capsula sound takes you on an aural journey through constellations into the heart of the shaking sun.
